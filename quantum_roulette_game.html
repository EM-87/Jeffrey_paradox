This game visualizes the Jeffrey Paradox: In quantum branching scenarios, external observers (Jeffrey) and branch-conditional observers (survivors) have different rational expectations about the same outcome.

Each round, reality branches into all possible outcomes. Your subjective experience follows only branches where you survive, creating a systematic bias in your expectations.
